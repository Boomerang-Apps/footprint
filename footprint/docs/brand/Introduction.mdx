# Footprint Brand Guidelines

**Tagline:** Your Story, Your Art — הסיפור שלך, האמנות שלך

## Mission

Footprint transforms personal photos into museum-quality art prints through AI-powered style transfer. We make fine art accessible, personal, and meaningful.

## Design Principles

- **Hebrew-First** — All interfaces are designed RTL with Hebrew as the primary language. English is supported as a secondary language.
- **Warm & Inviting** — Vibrant gradients and soft shadows create an approachable, premium feel that inspires creativity.
- **Clean & Focused** — Minimal chrome lets the art take center stage. Every element serves the creative journey.
- **Accessible & Inclusive** — WCAG AA contrast ratios, clear typography, and intuitive navigation for all users.

## Target Audience

Creative individuals in Israel who want to turn meaningful photos into beautiful art for their homes. Age 25-55, design-conscious, value personalization.

## Language & RTL

- **Primary language:** Hebrew (עברית)
- **Direction:** RTL (right-to-left)
- **Secondary language:** English
- All layouts, icons, and navigation flow right-to-left by default
